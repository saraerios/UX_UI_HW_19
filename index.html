My Work
NueTravel A travel mobile app concept aimed at organization. "You work hard. Your travel app should too." Presently, there is a lack of apps that provide travel information all in one place. Planning travel across multiple sites is inconvenient and difficult for users. NueTravel proposes to improve travel planning options for young adults and create a seamlessjourney.

Palette At present, the world lacks an app that may allow art patrons to access gallery information without extensive Google searches. Artists also lack access to information about multiple galleries in one space. Palette, a gallery finding app, was designed to connect artists, art patrons, and galleries to local happenings in the art space.
